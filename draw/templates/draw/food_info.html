Mochi Donuts by Jenny Kim


Soft, light, and fluffy, these mochi donuts are a family favorite! The first time I tried mochi donuts was in Japan. They were so different from typical American donuts, so I was shook. The chew that these donuts had was unlike anything I'd ever had before. I immediately knew that I needed to try to make these for myself. So I asked the store owner what his secret was, and he told me it was adding a bit of coffee powder to his dough, to help with a fermenting process. I decided to try this technique out in these donuts. Hope you guys enjoy!
